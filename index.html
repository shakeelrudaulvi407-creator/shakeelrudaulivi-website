About Shakeel Rudaulivi

Urdu Poet • Author • Literary Personality

Shakeel Rudaulivi ka talluq Rudauli Sharif, Zila Ayodhya (Uttar Pradesh) se hai. Aap Urdu adab ki duniya ka ek mohtaram naam, shayar, samaji kirdar aur awaami numainda rahe hain.

Aap Anjuman Tameer-e-Adab, Rudauli ke Sadr hain aur Urdu adab ke farogh ke liye musalsal khidmaat anjaam dete rahe hain.

Aap Nagar Palika Parishad Rudauli ke Vice Chairman bhi reh chuke hain aur teen martaba Sabhasad muntakhib hue.

1992 se adabi safar ka aaghaz hua aur aaj tak mushairon aur adabi mehfilon me musalsal shirkat karte aa rahe hain.
