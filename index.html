About The Author

Shakeel Rudaulivi

Shakeel Rudaulivi belongs to Rudauli Sharif, Ayodhya (Uttar Pradesh). He is an Urdu poet, literary personality, social worker and former public representative. He serves as the President of Anjuman Tameer-e-Adab, Rudauli and has actively promoted Urdu literature through mushairas, literary gatherings and social service. His poetry collection Sulagti Ret reflects love, emotions, spirituality and social consciousness.
