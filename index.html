About Shakeel Rudaulivi

Shakeel Rudaulivi

Shakeel Rudaulivi ka talluq Rudauli Sharif, Zila Ayodhya (Uttar Pradesh) se hai. Aap Urdu adab ki duniya ka ek mohtaram naam, shayar, samaji kirdar aur awaami numainda rahe hain. Bachpan hi se Urdu zabaan-o-adab se dilchaspi ne aap ko shayari ki taraf mutawajjeh kiya aur aap ne apne kalam ke zariye mohabbat, insani jazbaat, samaji masail aur roohani afkar ko khubsurti se pesh kiya.

Aap Anjuman Tameer-e-Adab, Rudauli ke Sadr (President) hain aur Urdu adab ke farogh ke liye musalsal khidmaat anjaam dete rahe hain. Iske ilawa aap Nagar Palika Parishad Rudauli ke Vice Chairman ke ohde par bhi fa'iz reh chuke hain.

Aap ko awaam ne teen martaba Sabhasad (Parshad) muntakhib karke apna aitimad diya. Aap ne awaami khidmat aur taraqqiyati kaamon mein hamesha sargarm kirdar ada kiya aur Zila Yojna Samiti ke intikhab mein bhi hissa liya.

1992 mein pehli baar apna kalam adabi mehfil mein pesh karne ke baad aap ne mushairon aur adabi nashiston mein mustaqil shirkat ki. Aap ka majmua-e-kalam "Sulagti Ret" Urdu shayari ke us safar ki numayandagi karta hai jahan jazbaat, tajurbaat aur zindagi ki haqeeqatein ek dilkash adabi andaaz mein saamne aati hain.

Shakeel Rudaulivi ka talluq ek moazzaz khandan se hai. Aap ke paanch bhai aur ek behen hain. Aap ki shaksiyat adab, samaji khidmat aur awaami rabte ka khubsurat imtizaj hai, jis ne aap ko Rudauli aur us ke gird-o-nawah mein ek mohtaram maqam ata kiya hai.
